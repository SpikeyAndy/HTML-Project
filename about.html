My journey in studying both Computer Engineering and Marketing has been both challenging and rewarding.

On one hand, studying Computer Engineering has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of how technology works and how it can be applied to solve real-world problems. On the other hand, studying Marketing has given me a unique perspective on how businesses operate and how they can leverage technology to innovate and create value for their customers.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I am an avid reader and enjoy exploring different genres of literature. I also love to travel and experience new cultures, which has allowed me to gain a broader perspective on the world and the people in it. Additionally, I am passionate about music and have played the guitar for several years.

Throughout my academic and personal experiences, I have developed a strong set of skills, including critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and creativity. I am excited to continue developing these skills and applying them in my future career.
